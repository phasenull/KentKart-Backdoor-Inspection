    Mobile Application

    kentkart Mobile Application provides passengers with a wide range of user-friendly solutions: from smart route planning to a variety of payment options.

    Our application allows topping up smart cards and paying with the NFC and QR code technologies. Passengers can easily check approaching buses and other useful route information, plan their trips and see the nearest top-up points. Our smart route planning feature gives users an opportunity to compare all possible alternatives and get from one place to another in the fastest and cost-effective way.

    kentkart Mobile Application also offers intelligent solutions with a voice warning system for the visually impaired.

    Benefits of the Mobile Application

    Online Recharge

    QR Payments

    Passenger Information

    Online Recharge and Mobile Payments

    Passengers can easily top up their city cards using the application at any time, any place. They do not have to look for a dealer or worry that they will face an insufficient balance problem anymore.

    kentkart Mobile Application can be downloaded for free from Google Play and App Store. Moreover, after purchasing a physical or virtual card, it allows passengers to pay with the QR Code or NFC technology without needing a card.
